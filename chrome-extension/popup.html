Portal Credential Autofill v2.0
Behavior
Auto-submit login form after fill
Settings saved
Permission needed: requires access to inject credentials.
Click-to-fill No stored passwords Domain verified Audit logged Auto-erase
How it works:
1. Log in to the CRM and copy your sessionid cookie
2. When you visit a portal login page, a banner will appear
3. Click Fill Credentials to auto-fill username & password
4. Passwords are fetched on-demand and erased from memory immediately after fill

Security: Passwords are never stored in the extension. Credentials are only fetched when you click Fill on an approved portal domain.
