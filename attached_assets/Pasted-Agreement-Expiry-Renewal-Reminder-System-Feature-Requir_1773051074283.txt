Agreement Expiry & Renewal Reminder System
Feature Requirement + Email Template
Objective

Implement an automated Agreement Expiry Notification System to ensure no university/provider agreements expire without renewal. The system must send automatic reminder notifications before the agreement expiry date and also notify if renewal is delayed.

This helps prevent commission loss, compliance issues, and partnership disruption.

1. Trigger Logic

The system must monitor the Agreement Expiry Date in the agreement database.

Reminder Schedule

Send automatic reminders at the following intervals:

Days Before Expiry	Action
90 Days	First reminder
60 Days	Second reminder
30 Days	Third reminder
14 Days	Urgent reminder
7 Days	Critical reminder
Expired	Expiry notification
2. Renewal Delay Alert

If Agreement Status = "Renewal in Progress" and the agreement expires before renewal is completed:

Trigger Renewal Delay Alert.

Send reminder every 7 days until renewal is completed.

3. Email Recipients
