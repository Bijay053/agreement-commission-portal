Below are professional email templates for Agreement Expiry Reminders for your University Agreement Management System.
These templates are written so the system can automatically send them to:

au@studyinfocentre.com

info@studyinfocentre.com

partners@studyinfocentre.com

The system should dynamically insert agreement details using placeholders.

Email Templates for Agreement Expiry Reminders
Dynamic Fields (Placeholders)

Developer should support the following variables:

{university_name}

{agreement_title}

{territory_country}

{start_date}

{expiry_date}

{days_remaining}

{portal_link}

{primary_contact_name}

{primary_contact_email}

{primary_contact_phone}

1. 90 Days Before Expiry
Subject

Agreement Expiry Reminder – {university_name} ({days_remaining} Days Remaining)

Email Body

Dear Team,

This is an automated reminder that the following university agreement is approaching its expiry date.

Agreement Details:

University: {university_name}
Agreement Title: {agreement_title}
Territory: {territory_country}
Agreement Start Date: {start_date}
Agreement Expiry Date: {expiry_date}
Days Remaining: {days_remaining}

Primary University Contact:

Name: {primary_contact_name}
Email: {primary_contact_email}
Contact Number: {primary_contact_phone}

Please review the agreement and initiate renewal discussions if required.

You may review the agreement securely in the portal using the link below:

{portal_link}

Kindly ensure renewal discussions are initiated well before the expiry date to avoid any interruption to recruitment activities.

Regards
Agreement Management System
Study Info Centre
